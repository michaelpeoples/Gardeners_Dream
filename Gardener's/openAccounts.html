What type of account would you like to open?

What type of checking account would you like to open?

The simple checking is an account with no monthly service charge. A great place to start if you're unsure and an easy to maintain account that includes all the essentials.

The interest checking is an interest bearing account that requires a $500 average daily balance. If the average daily balance is not met, there is a $2.00 service charge assessed on the last day of the month. However, with this option the customer receives two free boxes of checks at the time the account is opened.

What type of Savings account would you like to open?

Our savings is a flexible account that only requires $100.00 to open. An easy way to start saving without having to worry about keeping a large balance.

Our Money Market account offers an interest based on the amount in the account. The more you save, the more you make!

Additional Information

Bank Card:

You will receive your new bank cards in the mail within 3-5 business days. A pin may be set up by calling the number on the back of your new card and entering in your social security number.

Funding Account:

You may visit the transfers page after the new account has been created and fund your new account. You may also visit any local branch and make a deposit into your new account.
